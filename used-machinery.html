Common Questions

Buying Used Machinery from China

What types of used machinery do you sell?

We supply used excavators, wheel loaders, bulldozers, motor graders, road rollers, skid steer loaders and more — all sourced and inspected through our network in China before export.

How do you verify the working hours?

We provide on-site video showing the dashboard meter reading during the engine start test. Hour readings on modern ECU-controlled machines are much harder to tamper with than on older mechanical meters.

Can I arrange a third-party inspection?

Yes. All machines are at our physical yard in Shanghai. You can arrange SGS, BV, or any independent inspector at any time before purchase. We welcome it.

What shipping terms are available?

We offer FOB Shanghai, CIF (to any port), and EXW. Most buyers in Africa and the Middle East prefer CIF for simplicity. Container, flat-rack, and RORO options are all available.

How long does shipping take to Africa or Middle East?

Typical transit times: West Africa (Nigeria, Ghana) 20–28 days; East Africa (Kenya, Tanzania) 18–25 days; Middle East (UAE, Saudi Arabia) 12–18 days from FOB Shanghai.

Do you provide export documents?

Yes — full export documentation including commercial invoice, packing list, bill of lading, and certificate of origin. We have experience exporting to 50+ countries with no issues.
