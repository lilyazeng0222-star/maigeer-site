About Maigeer
Machinery

MAIGEER Machinery is a used construction machinery supplier based in Shanghai, China. We provide reliable machines, real photos, working videos and export support for global buyers.

Reliable Used Machines
for Global Projects

We focus on supplying used excavators, bulldozers, motor graders, road rollers, backhoe loaders, dump trucks and other heavy equipment for contractors and dealers worldwide.

Our goal is simple: help buyers find suitable machines with clear condition information, competitive prices and professional export service.

  • Real machine photos and working videos available
  • Inspection support before shipment
  • Multiple brands and machine types available
  • FOB / CIF shipping support worldwide
  • Direct communication — no middlemen
